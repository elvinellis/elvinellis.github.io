    Goodbye Mercedes
    9 February

    This is my Dad’s Mercedes which we use it like a family car. I’ve grown to embrace its quirks over the past three years. When you step on the foot brake after parking, it rocks back and forth. In full reverse and full steering lock, the engine power drops like ninety percent. At the end of its ten-years life, it is still looking good.
