    2 September, Saturday - I hate Broccoli + Friends Visit

    Why the “Sorry Mommy” note? That’s because Audrey headbutt Ellis’ eye yesterday and it hurt like hell. Therefore, Audrey should apologise. This is her handwritten apology with some help from me. With a teary face, all will be forgiven.
    Every child on earth hates broccoli. I hated them when I was young. I had no idea that an infant at seven months will be disgusted by it. Audrey scrunched her face up when Ellis stuffed the broccoli stalk into her mouth. She had been playing with the broccoli and cannot stand to put them near her mouth. The smell is just offending to her. I conclude that it is human instinct to reject broccoli. That gave me idea that every parent will want to buy broccoli-flavoured remote controllers. Or maybe a broccoli-scented spray. A quick spray on the objects you don’t want your child to bite. I will totally buy that.
    Just wondering — is broccolus the singular form of broccoli?
    We had guests in the evening. Baby Sophie is here to visit us. Audrey is excited to meet her new friend. Watching infants interact with one another is fascinating. They do not have any language skills so all communications is through movements, facial expressions and screams. They still get along somehow. As parents, we are afraid our baby will hurt another as they grab, pinch, slash, scratch with the undeveloped gross motor skill.
