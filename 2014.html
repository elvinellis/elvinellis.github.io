January 2014
e bought a house at the start of the year. As the house will only be ready in 2016, we focused on the next important thing — getting married. There was a sense of excitement as we discussed about our wedding plan. It was dating every weekend, except we were visiting different hotels to see their venue and offers and Ellis will try out wedding dresses in bridal shops. At last, we decided on Swissotel Merchant Court and Sophia Wedding. We also picked an auspicious date of 27 June 2015 to have our wedding. With the big day set in stone, all activities and planning was worked out backwards from that. Our next favourite part was the pre-wedding photoshoot in October. To brainstorm for ideas on outdoor settings, we visited different cafes, marketplace, and places of interest in Singapore. I like nature and Ellis loves flowers so we settled for the iconic Botanical Gardens. Next, I like the Volkswagen Kombi and Ellis loves ice-cream so Ben’s and Jerry at Dampsey Hill became the second outdoor setting. When we received the photos, we were elated. Our photographer, Ryan, did a stellar job of capturing the beauty of a couple in love.

Air Show
15 February

The annual event of the Air Show is taking place this weekend. It is an exhibition for aero-engineering and air defense companies to showcase their products and rub shoulders. For us commoners, we are just here to view our RSAF’s fleet. It is only on this day that the public get touch these classified equipments and take photos with them. I get to do silly things like sticking my head into the behind of an F16. As for Ellis, she is just happy she gets to drink Tiger Radler on this sunny day.
This is the iHawk ground-to-air missiles loaded onto the launcher. Every part of this machine is designed to withstand the tremendous force when the missile fires off.
The chinook is helicopter designed for quick air lifting.

India Shillim
28 September

My company had a retreat for all employees for its anniversay celebration. It was a beautiful place in India called Shillim. On the second day, I signed up for a hiking trip up the hill with other colleagues. After a gruelling bash through the forest upwards, we made it to the top. I did not expect such a spectacular sight to greet me. There were long grasses everywhere that swayed to the strong gusty winds. We laid on the grasses like a sofa and breathed in the fresh air.

Pre-wedding Photoshoot
8 October

Suit — checked; Dress — checked; Shoes — checked; Hair wax — checked; Props — checked; Make-up artist — checked; Photographer — checked; Candy — checked. We are ready to rock and roll for our pre-wedding photoshoot. In the morning, Ryan took our photos in the studio. Then we had lunch packed from the nearby Maxwell center. Ellis had a change in hairdo. The remainder of the afternoon was outdoor shoot. We travelled to Ben’s and Jerry at Dempsey Hill first. Ellis was so pretty in her make-up and dress that I kept sticking my tongue into her mouth when Ryan requested for kissing shots. Next, we headed over to Botanical Gardens where there is the iconic bandstand and yellow raintrees. Our last place was at the Sentosa beach where we played with bubbles to create a dreamy world.
