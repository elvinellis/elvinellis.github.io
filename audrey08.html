    21 October, Saturday - Brainfit session

    70 cm 8.8 Kg
    A fun-filled, action-packed day to remember. Honestly, I was quite excited as time leads to this day. Today, Audrey will be attending her first ever class with Brainfit and we will be celebrating Papa’s birthday at some special place.
    After our weekend morning routine, we prepared ourselves and went to Goldhill Centre at Novena to attend a trial class with Brainfit. I attended the class with Audrey while Papa attended the seminar held by the founder.
    The lesson began with a 15min softplay and socialising. Audrey was enjoying herself in that colourfully classroom and was also constantly observing what other kids were doing in the room. She understood the instruction but she did not have much reaction. However, she could not contain her excitement as she began crawling around the softplay. It got me thinking that we should probably get her something related to such activity for her birthday.
    The class continued to another room and the kids (with parents) sat together in a circle and a variety of activities were held. We played with toys made up with bowls and shakers to invoke creative playing in them. There were art and craft session and storytelling session. I think Audrey enjoyed herself and I am glad she managed to interact with other kids and the teachers around.

    21 October, Saturday - Indochine Supertree Dinner

    We went to Gardens by the Bay after the class...for Papa’s birthday dinner. And the place is… Indochine Supertree! Woohoo‼ Audrey got to sit with us on her baby chair while we ordered our food. Lucky us, Grandma cooked porridge for Audrey so she can eat with us!
    After settling her and thanks to Gerber puffs, we got to eat together. The appetizer was a lychee and kurobuta pork roll. It was a good appetizer to begin with. Nice with a tinch of lychee. We got ourselves a steam cod with tiger prawns and a seafood trio containing juicy scallops, prawns and tiger prawns. We had made a great choice because they were so fresh and delicious. Totally worth the price. We proceeded with some wines and dessert. A reconstructed sticky rice mango on magnum ice cream. It was surprisingly good! The restaurant was really nice too. A complimentary matcha cheese cake as a birthday cake for Papa.
    We really enjoyed ourselves. After the dinner, we went up to its rooftop bar. Lol. That was Audrey’s maiden trip to a pub! The night view was amazing and we took some wefies to end the night. Happy Birthday Papa! Hope you like what we have prepared for you!
