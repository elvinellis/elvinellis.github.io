National Day Parade Rehearsal
1 August

We got tickets to the parade rehearsal. Although this is not the actual day, the crowd, including us, was visibly excited. This is the Jubilee year of the founding of Singapore. We painted the atmosphere in red and white - the national colors. In our goodie bag, we had a cap, scarf, clapper and flag. From the floating platform at the Marina Bay, we got a good view of the sky and a wide screen of the crowd over at the Padang.
The highlight of the show is the awesome air display. There are F-16s, chinooks, C-130 and many others. A clear blue sky, fluffy clouds, setting sun and silhouettes of flying aircrafts makes for a nice picture.
Nothing beats clips of the event. Enjoy!

Istana Open House
2 August

Cloud Forest
16 August

The cloud forest is in the taller dome of the two conservatories in gardens by the bay. At the entrance, you are greeted by the roar of the waterfall as large volume of water is thrown off the three storeys artificial cliff. More than being aesthetic, the falling water creates a natural dampness in the air, the kind of humidity you will find in the high attitude tropical forest.

Flower Dome
8 August

The flower dome is packed with people today. Under this artificial dome of steel and glass, flowers and trees impossible to find in a tropical climate grow in a temperature regulated environment. The flowers imported from around the world are unusual. Some of them challenge the definition of what a flower should be.
The orchids are an bio engineering marvel - horticulturists create colors and patterns by selecting the specific genes.
The standout feature for me are the century-old olive trees that were shipped in. They were bearing yellow flowers when we visited. If you stand still and breathe deeply, you can make out the scent of olive bread.

The Edge and Equilibrium
24 October

We are celebrating my birthday today. Ellis reserved a table at the Edge. They have a wonderful spread of food across the floor. Seated in our cosy corner, we sampled different cuisines. I'm a fan of artisan bread and I got a tasty black bread that looks like a piece of charcoal.
Ellis is looking fabulous today. Having my beautiful wife beside me on my birthday is all the present I need.
Blow the candle, make a wish and cut the cake. A year older now.
After the meal, we went shopping at Suntec. Walking aounrd mindlessly in our own world. At Marina Square, we took pictures of the pavement. Few people would have walked on the outside, even lesser would have noticed the tiles of this pavement. Every small tile was laid by hand. Where the pattern curves, odd shapes have to be cut out of the tiles to fill the gap. This is a work of an artist. We don't have things like this anymore.
It was night when we got to the Capitol building. It was newly renovated. The neon lights framing the words reminded us of the heritage of the building. More impressive is the water fountain. Lights from the ground illuminates the water columns as it shoots out. Inside the building, we sat down for drinks and snacks at the Equilibrium. The cocktails here have interesting contoction and names.
