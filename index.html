    nce upon a time, there was a boy, Elvin, and a girl, Ellis. Amongst the myriad possibilities of what life should have been, their story became inextricably interwined when they met. Thus began a steady rhythm of courtships and explorations. She fell in love with his vast inner world and took up a special place in it. He was captivated by her beauty and wants to spend his whole life with her. Although fleeting in comparison to the age of Time, their moments together are truly special to them only. They went on merry adventures and collected the world in their thumping hearts. He was king and she was queen…
    Here, under the stars and there too, under the clouds, everywhere with you.
