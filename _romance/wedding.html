Wedding Videos

Reaching Ellis' house at 6.30am, Elvin and his Brothers (Ben, Augu, Darius and Tzyy Yih) went through the gate-crash trials carefully thought out by the bridesmaids (Candy, Jan, Fraemone and Swee Wah). After winning Princess Ellis' heart, Elvin brought her back to his home. It was a hive of activities at Elvin's house. Mum, Dad, Aunts, Uncles and cousins were served tea. After the tea ceremony, everyone made their way to the Swissotel Merchant Court. At 11am, families and friends gathered at the pool-side gazebo for the solemnisation ceremony.

Our Vows

 

“ I, Elvin Chua, on this day take you to be my wife, my soulmate and my love.To carry on this journey that we have started, hand in hand until we are old.I promise to support you in times of trouble and laugh with you in joyous moments. To be faithful to our love and see you only with respect.To challenge you to be a better person and allow you to do the same for me.
I give you all the love and compassion my whole life long.”

“Today, I, Ellis, take you, Elvin as my husband. Now we will feel no rain, for each of us will be shelter for the other. Now there will be no loneliness, for each of us will be companion to the other. There is only one life before us, and our seasons will be good and long.
I promise to love, honor, and cherish you, no matter what lies before us, for as long as we both shall live. ”
