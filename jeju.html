Korean Cuisine

Korean dishes are colorful - red, white, green, yellow, brown, black, and white. They come in many small dishes spread across the table. I especially like the kimchi, seaweed and fat bean sprouts. Good chopstick skill is must for an enjoyable eating experience. Another favourite style of eating is the hotpot soup filled to the brim with vegetables, mushroom and meat. Breakfast tends to be congee and hot buns.
